Title        : Lady Snowblood                                  
IMDb URL     : http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0158714/            
Director     : Toshiya Fujita                                  
Genre        : Drama/Action                                    
User Rating  : 8.2/10                                          
Plot Outline : Yuki's family is nearly wiped out before she is 
                born due to the machinations of a band of criminals. These      
                criminals kidnap and brutalize her mother but leave her alive. 
                Later her mother ends up in prison with only revenge to keep  
                her alive. She creates an instrument for this revenge by       
                purposefully getting pregnant. Though she dies in childbirth,  
                she makes sure that the child will be raised as an assassin to  
                kill the criminals who destroyed her family. Young Yuki never  
                knows the love of a family but only killing and revenge.
