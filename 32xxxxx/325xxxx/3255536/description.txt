Getaway in Stockholm 5 
Mr X, the worlds most famous traffic violator is back in town! This time in a car well known from the movie The Fast and the Furious, a supertuned Mazda Rx-7 pushed to the limit. Together with a friend in a just as fast supercar they show that after sunset in Stockholm another life begins where they own the streets, sometimes at speeds up to 300 km/h. Watch this great documentary of awesome fast cars, streetracing, car stunts, races and babes - fasten your seatbelts and enjoy the ride!
