A Hong Kong variation on Robin Hood. The corrupt officials of a 
Chinese village are continually robbed by a masked bandit know as 
"Iron Monkey" named after a benevolent deity. When all else fails, 
the Govenor forces a traveling physician (Donnie Yen) into finding 
the bandit. The arrival of an evil Shaolin monk, brings the Physician 
and Iron Monkey together to battle the corrupt government.
