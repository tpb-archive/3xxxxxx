Story :

A young monk catches a train to a temple in a hill. On the way, he meets Pon and Tangmo, two people who trying to escape from their personal problems. Later, the threesome becomes friend, but they have to separate to find their goals. The young monk arrives the temple and experiences to many unusual senior monks, while Pon and Tangmo meet each other again in a village and begin their relationship. But, peaceful life of Pon and Tangmo is getting in trouble when a stranger guy steps in, and sets a chaos over the village and the temple.
